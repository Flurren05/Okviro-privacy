Privacy Policy for Okviro

Effective date: June 25, 2026

Okviro ("the app", "we", "us") is built to keep your data on your device. We do not run servers that collect your personal information, and the app has no account system, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

Information we do not collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to us or to any third party. Okviro works entirely on your device.

Data that stays on your device

  • Widget settings (text, colors, fonts, and similar choices) are stored locally on your device so your widgets can render.
  • Photos you choose for a photo widget remain on your device and are never uploaded or shared.
  • Health (steps). With your permission, Okviro reads your step count through Apple HealthKit to show it in a widget. This data is read on your device, cached locally, and is never transmitted to us, sold, shared, or used for advertising or any purpose other than displaying it in your widget.
  • Location. With your permission, Okviro uses your location on your device to calculate sunrise/sunset times and to show a map widget. Your location is not stored on our servers, transmitted to us, or shared.

You can revoke Health or Location access at any time in the iOS Settings app.

Purchases

Subscriptions ("Okviro Pro") are processed by Apple through your Apple Account. We never receive or store your payment details. Apple's handling of purchase information is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

Third parties

Okviro does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs, and does not share data with third parties.

Children

Okviro is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Data retention and deletion

Because all of your data lives on your device, deleting the app removes it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
