Common questions

  • My widget isn't updating. iOS refreshes widgets on its own schedule to save battery, so changes can take a little while to appear. Removing and re-adding the widget usually refreshes it right away.
  • I can't see my photos or location/weather. Make sure you allowed Okviro access in the iOS Settings app (Settings > Okviro), then re-open the app.
  • Restoring a purchase. Open Okviro, go to the Okviro Pro screen, and tap "Restore Purchases" while signed in with the Apple Account you bought it on.
